Design for Social Good

promoting higher edcuation through particiaptory design

The deliverable was a three day hands on curriculum where third grade students designed a university. To do so they had to learn about applying to college and the different types of majors. I led a team of six teammates and thirty elemntary students in every stage of the design process.

Needfinding

Analyzed sociocultural context using publically accesible data, observations, interviews, and surveys for students and parents.

\

Created user personas to understand the needs of all stakeholders including principal, teacher, parents and the students.

Ideate

Compared existing innitiatives that promote access to higher education in underserved communities.

\

Developed fast and diverse ideas followed by a convergent process of considering the sustainability, affordability and usability of each idea.

Prototype and Test

Intereacted with students and present the curriculum to test their level of understanding.
